ABOUT ME

I am an industrial, information and interaction designer seeking, since the beginning of my career, opportunities for improving people’s conditions and relationships through collaborative approaches, with a specific focus in the design of objects, visualizations and experimental devices. I have always been attracted by complex projects and interdisciplinary teams, which has given me the opportunity to expand my knowledge of other areas and work in R&D, technology, culture, public museums and creative industries.

My interests include social problems, systemic thinking and the thriving intersection of design and engineering as an instrument to explore interfaces in the digital and tangible worlds, where I get to study, research and implement innovative solutions and strategies that aim to intervene spaces, generate unexpected behaviors and develop meaningful experiences in different contexts.
